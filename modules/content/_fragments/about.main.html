Our Story

A premium import house, built for Bangladesh.

GulfRabit exists to make the world’s best products arrive in Bangladesh exactly as they should — authentic, intact, and fast.

Sourced. Verified. Delivered.

We started with a simple frustration: imported goods in this market were either hard to trust or hard to find. Counterfeits, broken cold-chains, and grey-market stock made premium shopping a gamble. GulfRabit is our answer — a single, considered storefront where every item is sourced from a known origin, verified for authenticity, and delivered with the care it deserves.

From Jordanian Medjool dates and New Zealand milk powder to Japanese audio and industrial-grade PCBs, we curate across worlds most stores would never put under one roof — because our customers live in all of them.
